[Horizontal, labelwidth=33]
Root element:: `vaadin-accordion`


=== Panels
[Horizontal, labelwidth=33]
Panel element:: `vaadin-accordion-panel`
Opened panel:: `vaadin-accordion-panel+++<wbr>+++**[opened]**`
Disabled panel:: `vaadin-accordion-panel+++<wbr>+++**[disabled]**`
Panel header (a.k.a. summary):: `vaadin-accordion-panel+++<wbr>+++**::part(summary)**`
Toggle:: `vaadin-accordion-panel+++<wbr>+++**::part(toggle)**`
Toggle icon:: `vaadin-accordion-panel+++<wbr>+++**::part(toggle)::before**`
Panel header content wrapper:: `vaadin-accordion-panel+++<wbr>+++**::part(summary-content)**`
Panel body content wrapper:: `vaadin-accordion-panel+++<wbr>+++**::part(content)**`
